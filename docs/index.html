Overview

RedactFlow is built to help users blur photos on iPhone, redact screenshots, pixelate video on iOS, and hide sensitive details before sharing.

Data Collection

RedactFlow does not collect personal data, does not require an account, and does not include analytics, ads, or third-party trackers.

Photos Access

The app requests Photos access only to let users import selected photos or videos and save edited exports back to the photo library.

On-Device Processing

All image and video redaction happens locally on the iPhone. Media is not uploaded to remote servers.

Data Sharing

RedactFlow does not sell, share, or disclose user media to third parties.
