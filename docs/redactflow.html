What RedactFlow Does

RedactFlow helps users hide personal information in photos and videos with blur, pixelate, or black bar tools. It is useful for screenshots before sharing, content creators cleaning visuals, and anyone who needs to hide personal info in photos, blur faces on iPhone, or remove visible names, emails, and addresses from media.

FAQ

How to blur photos on iPhone?

You can blur photos on iPhone with RedactFlow by selecting an image, placing a rectangular redaction area over the private section, choosing Blur, and exporting the result back to Photos.

How to hide sensitive information in screenshots?

To hide sensitive details in screenshots, use RedactFlow to cover names, emails, account numbers, or addresses with blur, pixelate, or black bars before sharing.

How to pixelate a video on iOS?

RedactFlow helps users pixelate video on iOS by letting them pick a video, place redaction regions over the visible private area, and export the edited clip.
